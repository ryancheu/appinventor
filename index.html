App Inventor Demo

I've created this page to demonstrate my work on App Inventor. Use the download zip link to download tutorial.aia and tutorial.apk as a zip.

This application is pretty simple--right now it just shows a ball bouncing (bouncing is not perfect against ImageSprites) and a counter at the bottom showing the total number of collisions with a sprite. I also made the ball decelerate over time and added sounds.
